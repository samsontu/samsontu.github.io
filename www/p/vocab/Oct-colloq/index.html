A Case Study in Collaboration

Project #1: The vocabulary server of the InterMed Project.

Project #2: The ontology server of the Knowledge Sharing Technology Project.

In the spirit of last month's SMI retreat at Asilomar, I will focus this talk on inter-project collaboration. Over the past year, the InterMed project has had a generally successful collaboration with the Knowledge Sharing Technology Project at Welch Rd. In order to understand this collaboration, I will present summary descriptions of these two projects. One of the aims of the talk will be to explain why we are interested in "ontologies" and in the tools and infrastructure provided by the Knowledge Sharing Technology Project. I will describe the benefits to the InterMed vocabulary server project, and more generally, why Medical Informaticians should be interested in such tools.
