The InterMed Vocabulary Server Group

This page contains pointers to versions of the InterMed Vocabulary. This content is being developed collaboratively by researchers at Columbia University, Harvard University and the University of Utah.

Of course, all items here are subject to frequent revision. That's the whole point of internet collaboration. Our vocabularies are currently stored within

The Ontology Server

(Instructions for new InterMed users)

See, for example the ontologies: "intermed-Jan96", "loinc-orig" (warning: very large), "Thelper-Drugs", "Med-th-Drugs", "intermed-vocab" (not a complete vocabulary!) and "mbta-drugs". The ontology server is a running process on the machine www-ksl.svc.stanford.edu. If you have questions or problems, send email to gennari@smi.stanford.edu or ontology-librarian@ksl.stanford.edu.
