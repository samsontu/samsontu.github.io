Distinguishing Characteristics: Clinical Trials (CT) versus Guidelines

  • Commonality:

    • CT versus guidelines? Subset? Superset? Intersection? Specialization?
    • Many common elements that vary in degree of importance (decision-making, temporal issues)

  • Distinguishing characteristics:

    • Strong scientific design
    • Complex visit, intra-visit-tasks, inter-visit tasks sched.
    • Randomization (patients, providers, hospitals)
    • Data gathering critical; requirements are regulated
    • Workflow compliance is regulated
