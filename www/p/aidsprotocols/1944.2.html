4.2 Exclusion Criteria

4.21 Individuals with a past history of ddI or ddC use for > 30 days within the preceding year or at any time within 3 months of study entry.

4.22 History of acute or chronic pancreatitis, gout, or uric acid nephropathy.

4.23 Medical contraindication or is felt to be non-compliant in the opinion of the investigator.

4.24 Peripheral neuropathy >= Grade 2 (Appendix IIIB).

4.25 Pregnant or lactating women.

4.26 Patients receiving acute therapy for an infection or other medical illness. Acute therapy must have been completed for 14 days prior to study entry.

4.27 Receiving a prohibited medication (see Section 5.51).
