5.0 STUDY POPULATION

Patients at high risk for recurrent Candida vaginitis will be enrolled to the trial including those patients who have a history of >= 2 episodes of Candida vaginitis (at least one episode must be documented by a health care specialist) occurring within 6 months before study entry, or who have acute vaginal candidiasis within 10 days of study entry.

A total number of 70 women will be evaluated. Patients with persistent disease or who relapse during the follow-up for the treatment study may also be eligible for this prophylactic treatment study. Patients with mixed infections will be treated accordingly (Appendix A).
