4.2 CONCURRENT MEDICATIONS

4.21 Antiretroviral agents(s) and PCP prophylaxis are strongly recommended. It is recommended that those patients on AZT take 500-600 mg. daily. Daily trimethoprim-sulfamethoxazole (1 double strength) or dapsone (100 mg) are also recommended.

4.22 Erythropoietin and colony stimulating factor therapies are allowed.

4.23 Fluconazole will be discontinued if another azole or amphotericin B is required.

4.24 Treatment with oral troches or nystatin is discouraged, but allowed.

4.25 Co-treatment in other studies is allowed pending approval of the site-specific investigator.
