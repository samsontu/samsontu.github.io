3.2 EXCLUSION CRITERIA

3.21 Patients who have symptoms of ARC and CDC class IV diseases. (See Appendix IV)

3.22 Patients with a history of pancreatitis.

3.23 Symptomatic peripheral neuropathy (Grade >= 2 by ACTG criteria - see Appendix II).

3.24 Patients who are known active illicit drug or alcohol abusers.

3.25 Patients who have had previous therapy with nucleoside or non-nucleoside antiretroviral agents, other than ZDV (for example, ddI, ddC, FLT, 3TC, d4T, L697661, TIBO, etc).

3.26 Patients with chronic diarrhea defined as > 4 stools (loose or watery) per day for at least 4 of the 7 days immediately prior to screening.

3.27 Women who are pregnant, and/or who intend to breast feed during the study.

3.28 Patients receiving systemic cytotoxic chemotherapy or radiation therapy (other than limited localized therapy to the skin).

3.29 Patients who, in the opinion of the investigator, are receiving therapy or have other medical conditions, that is felt to have a relative medical contraindication.
