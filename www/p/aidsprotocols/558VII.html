VII. MANAGEMENT OF DRUG TOXICITY/CHANGING ANTI-RETROVIRAL DRUGS

A. Subjects may re-institute, discontinue or change anti-retroviral therapy as clinically indicated, at their own or at their physician's discretion. In addition, they may elect to participate in other experimental anti-retroviral protocols. Such changes will be treated as a dependent variable in the intention to treat analysis.

B. Other medications may also be changed as clinically indicated, at the discretion of the subject or [original text reads "of"] the subject's physician.
