6.0 Randomization

Randomization will be blinded to the individual investigators. A blocked randomization scheme will be computer-generated in blocks of 4 for each of the 4 centers. Patients will be stratified according to whether their CD4 count is greater than 200 or less than or equal to 200/mm^3 at entry. Once a patient has been enrolled, the individual investigator may contact the central study site for randomization, verify that the entry criteria have been satisfied, and receive randomization. Blinded drug will be distributed to each of the study centers before randomization.
