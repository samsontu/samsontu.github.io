3.2 Exclusion Criteria

3.21 Known adverse reactions to sulfa, trimethoprim or TMP/SMX.

3.22 Prior receipt of TMP/SMX as primary prophylaxis for PCP (prior use of aerosolized pentamidine and dapsone is allowed).

3.23 Initiation of anti-infective agents including TMP/SMX (for indications other than PCP prophylaxis) or antiretroviral agents within 4 weeks prior to study entry.

3.24 Use of prohibited medicines within 2 weeks prior to study entry. These include antihistamines, procysteine, glutathione, N-acetylcysteine (NAC), systemic corticosteroids (except those used for replacement purposes), leucovorin calcium (folinic acid), TMP and sulfa drugs.

3.25 Inability to comply with the dosing schedule or follow the instructions for completing the dosing record.

3.26 Nursing (breast-feeding) mothers are ineligible.
