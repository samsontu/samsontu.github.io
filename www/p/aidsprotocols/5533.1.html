3.0 PATIENT SELECTION

3.1 Inclusion Criteria

1. HIV +

2. Age > 12 years

3. Patients with serum cryptococcal antigen >= 1:8, positive extraneural cryptococcal culture, or histologic evidence of cryptococcus will receive immediate treatment; patients with serum cryptococcal antigen < 1:8 repeated x 2, at least 24 hours apart, and negative cultures will be entered onto the observational component of the trial.

4. Signed informed consent

5. May have been on antifungal prophylaxis up to 200 mg/d with fluconazole, ketoconazole, or itraconazole.
