4.2 Exclusion Criteria

1. Monotherapy with clarithromycin, azithromycin, clofazimine, or ethambutol for > 1 month of cumulative treatment (for each agent) prior to enrollment.

2. Combination therapy for > 72 hours with 2 or more agents active against MAC (does not include INH or pyrazinamide) for treatment of MAC in the proceeding month before enrollment*.

3. Combination therapy as defined under #2 above for treatment of MAC for > 2 weeks if this occurred more than one month prior to enrollment.

4. Life expectancy < 8 weeks.

5. Unwillingness to take medications as prescribed.

6. History of severe allergy to macrolide antimicrobials (Type 1 hypersensitivity, Stevens Johnson syndrome, or exfoliative dermatitis).

7. Patient is currently on active therapy with immune modulators.

a) Colony stimulating factors (GCSF, GMCSF)

b) Systemic alpha or gamma interferon Erythropoietin (Eprex) is allowed.

8. Chest radiograph suggestive of active tuberculosis.

9. Patient is currently on active therapy with carbamazapine or theophylline, unless the investigator agrees to carefully monitor blood levels.

10. Patient may not be enrolled if receiving investigational drug(s) without approval of protocol chair or designated team member.

* These patients must be off non-study therapy for at least 72 hours and have repeat blood cultures before they begin study therapy.
