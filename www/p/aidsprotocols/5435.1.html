5.1 Inclusion criteria (all of the following must be true):

5.11 HIV positive female.

5.12 Presumed (on the basis of symptoms and positive KOH smear) or documented (on the basis of positive Sabouraud culture) vaginal Candida infection.

5.13 Age greater than or equal to 12 years.

5.14 Women of child-bearing potential should use an effective method of contraception (e.g., condoms and foam), and have a negative pregnancy test at baseline.

5.15 Laboratory parameters

5.151 Hemoglobin >= 8.0g/dl

5.152 Absolute neutrophil count > = 500/mm^3

5.153 Platelets >= 50,000

5.154 Creatinine < 3X upper limit of normal

5.155 Bilirubin < 3X upper limit of normal

5.156 SGOT(AST), SGPT(ALT), alkaline phosphatase <5X upper limit of normal

5.16 Patient is willing to give informed consent and to comply with the protocol.
