2.0 STUDY OBJECTIVES

2.1 Primary

To determine whether the gradual initiation of TMP/SMX reduces the incidence of treatment-limiting adverse reactions compared to the routine initiation of the drug for PCP prophylaxis in HIV-infected subjects.

2.2 Secondary

To determine whether the gradual initiation of TMP/SMX reduces the incidence of adverse reactions that are not treatment-limiting compared to the routine initiation of the drug.
