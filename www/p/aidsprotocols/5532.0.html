2.0 PURPOSE OF THE STUDY

2.1 Primary Objectives

1. To evaluate the safety and efficacy of fluconazole plus flucytosine in the treatment of extraneural cryptococcal disease.

2. To assess the efficacy of fluconazole plus flucytosine in preventing dissemination of cryptococcus in patients with antigenemia.

3. To assess if oral combination antifungal therapy is adequate in patients who have received prior fluconazole therapy.

4. To observe the natural history of patients with low titer cryptococcal antigen of uncertain significance and no evidence of clinical disease (observational component of the trial).

2.2 Secondary Objectives

1. To evaluate the development of resistance of cryptococcal organisms to fluconazole in patients receiving fluconazole prophylaxis.

2. To evaluate the sites of infection of cryptococcus in AIDS patients with asymptomatic cryptococcal antigenemia.
