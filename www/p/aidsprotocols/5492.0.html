2.0 Study Rationale

The next step in the development of therapies for MAC would be to determine if the addition of one or two drugs to a macrolide improves outcome. A logical approach would be to determine whether the addition of a second agent to a macrolide will prevent the high incidence of mycobacterial resistance and late recrudescence of MAC bacteremia and symptoms that was demonstrated with clarithromycin or whether a three drug regimen is needed.
