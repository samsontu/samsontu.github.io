4.2 Exclusion Criteria (from Section 4.0)

4.21 Patients with either a current or prior AIDS-related opportunistic infection, AIDS dementia, AIDS-wasting syndrome, or an AIDS-related malignancy other than minimal Kaposi's sarcoma (KS) disease (see 4.11 and Appendix VI).

4.22 Patients with a history of intolerance to ZDV at <= 600 mg/d, ddI at <= 400 mg/d or ddC at <= 2.25 mg/d. For the purposes of this study, intolerance will be defined as the same recurrent toxicities requiring dose interruptions and dose reductions or permanent discontinuation of the drugs (other than grade 3 or 4 anemia if patients meet the criteria of section 4.141).

4.23 Patients with a history of acute or chronic pancreatitis.

4.24 Patients with current ETOH abuse by personal history or report from primary physician.

4.25 Patients with grade 2 or higher peripheral neuropathy (Appendix IX).

4.26 Patients with a malignancy requiring systemic therapy.

4.27 Pregnant women or women who are breast-feeding.

4.28 Patients receiving acute therapy for an infection or other medical illness. Acute therapy must have been completed for 14 days prior to the time of study entry.
