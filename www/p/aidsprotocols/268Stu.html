STUDY MANAGEMENT

All questions concerning this protocol should be sent via e-mail to ACTG.TEAM268. The appropriate team member will respond to your questions via e-mail with a "cc" to ACTG.TEAM268. A response should generally be received within 24 hours (Monday - Friday).

FOR EXEMPTIONS, CONCOMITANT MEDICATIONS, TOXICITY MANAGEMENT, CLINICAL MEDICAL MANAGEMENT, AND CO-ENROLLMENT, THE PROTOCOL CHAIR/PROTOCOL VICE CHAIR WILL RESPOND:

  • Send an e-mail message to ACTG.TEAM268 (ATTN.: Michael Para)

  • Include the PID, SID, and eligibility question numbers to be waived, if applicable

  • Give a brief relevant history

  • Give the date of expected randomization, if applicable

FOR QUESTIONS ABOUT INCLUSION/EXCLUSION CRITERIA, THE SCHEDULE OF EVALUATIONS, CASE REPORT FORMS, ENTERING WAIVERS FOR RANDOMIZATION EXEMPTIONS, TRANSFERS, AND DELINQUENCIES, AND OTHER DATA MANAGEMENT ISSUES, THE DATA MANAGER WILL RESPOND:

  • Send an e-mail message to ACTG.TEAM268 (ATTN.: Ann Walawander)

  • Include the PID and SID

  • Give a detailed description of the question

    NOTE: Eligibility waivers can be entered to allow randomization only after receipt of written documentation of the exemption from the Protocol Chair or Vice Chair.
