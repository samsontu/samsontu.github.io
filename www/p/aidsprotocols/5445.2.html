5.2 EXCLUSION CRITERIA

Any of the following excludes patient from the study:

5.21 A history of Type I hypersensitivity reaction to fluconazole, ketoconazole, miconazole, clotrimazole or itraconazole (a history of hives, angioedema, wheezing or anaphylaxis).

5.22 Clinical conditions necessitating oral or parenteral antifungal therapy (with amphotericin B, 5-fluorocytosine, itraconazole or ketoconazole). Topical antifungal therapy, e.g., troches is discouraged, but allowed.

5.23 Pregnant or nursing women.

5.24 Patients under evaluation or treatment for acute opportunistic infection. (Patients being treated for CMV retinitis may be enrolled in the study).

5.25 Patients with unexplained fevers, elevation in alkaline phosphatase, liver function tests, odynophagia for which the diagnosis of a fungal infection has not been excluded.
