Appendix C

Procedure for sending isolates:

1. Prepare fungal sub-isolates in Sabouraud slants (not plates) at room temperature.

2. Label isolates, including the following information:

Patient initials

Study number, including site and number of patient

Laboratory identification number

Name of organism

Site of isolation of organism - oral, rectal or vaginal

Date of isolation

Please include with you package:

Name and telephone number of laboratory personnel to contact

3. Send batched isolates on a monthly basis in appropriately-labeled slants in a double biohazard container with a biohazard label.

4. Send all isolates to the following address: Jeanette P. Sison, MD / Carol Kane, RN, Division of Infectious Disease, AIDS Program, Santa Clara Valley Medical Center, 751 S. Bascom Ave., San Jose, CA 95128
