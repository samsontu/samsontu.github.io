3.1 INCLUSION CRITERIA

3.11 Patients must be >= 13 years of age.

3.12 Patients must be able to give and understand written informed consent. Patients who are < 18 years of age must have a parent or guardian sign an informed consent form.

3.13 Enrollment of women is encouraged. Females of child bearing potential are eligible if a negative serum pregnancy test is documented within 14 days prior to study entry. All patients will be instructed in the practice of effective barrier contraceptive techniques and should understand the potential risks to a fetus related to participation in an experimental protocol.

3.14 Patients who are asymptomatic and whose CD4 counts are between 300 - 600 cells/mm^3 as determined on one count within 30 days prior to start of study medication.

3.15 Patients must have had >= one month and <= 2 years of uninterrupted ZDV monotherapy (>= 300 mg/day), immediately prior to screening.

3.16 Patients must be able to swallow capsules and tablets without difficulty.

3.17 Adequate baseline organ function including the following laboratory values within 30 days prior to the start of study medication:

3.171 Laboratory:

3.1711 Hemoglobin - >= 10 g/dl for males; 9.0 g/dl for females (in the absence of transfusion within the preceding two weeks).

3.1712 Absolute Neutrophil Count (ANC) - >= 1000/mm^3.

3.1713 Platelet count - >= 75,000/mm^3.

3.1715 AST/ALT and alkaline phosphatase - <= 5 x Upper Limit of Normal (ULN).

3.1716 Bilirubin - <= 2.5 mg/dl.

3.1717 Total serum amylase <= 1.5 times ULN. (If serum amylase is > 1.5 times ULN, lipase <= 1.5 times ULN or pancreatic amylase may be used to qualify.)

3.18 Karnofsky Performance Score of >= 60.

3.19 Plasma/serum PCR for codon 215 mutation is not demonstrated at screening.
