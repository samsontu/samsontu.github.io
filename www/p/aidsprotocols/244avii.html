APPENDIX VII VIROLOGY SPECIMEN STORAGE AND SHIPPING PROCEDURES

At screening and at baseline

Codon 215 mutation assay on plasma and PBMC

Two yellow-top tubes (ACD; acid-citrate-dextrose) on each subject every 8 weeks and sent by overnight mail to either

Stanford (ACTG sites) or Walter Reed (DOD sites)

Specimen bank storage

1) 5 x 1 ml aliquots of serum

2) 5 x 2 ml aliquots of plasma (obtained from ACD tubes)

3) 5 x 2-million cell aliquots of PBMC

HIV-1 co-culture

1) 5 million patient PBMC + 5 million PHA-stimulated donor PBMC in 3.0 ml (final volume) standard culture media in 16 x 125 mm screw cap culture tubes

2) 50% media-exchange twice weekly; 2 million donor PBMC added on day 7

3) 5 x 0.5 ml aliquots of a mixture of cultured cells and supernatant (PLPI; presumed low-passage isolate) stored at day 14. PLPI to be used for phenotypic resistance assay and for MT-2 cell assay

4) HIV-1 P24 antigen on supernatant at day 14
