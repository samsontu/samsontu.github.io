4.2 Concurrent Medications

4.21 Antiretroviral agent(s) and PCP prophylaxis are strongly recommended. It is recommended that those patients on AZT take at least 500-600 mg daily. Daily trimethoprim-sulfamethoxazole (1 double strength) or dapsone (100 mg) are also recommended.

4.22 Erythropoietin and colony stimulating factor therapies are allowed.

4.23 Fluconazole will be discontinued if another azole or amphotericin B is required.

4.24 Treatment with oral troches is discouraged, but allowed.

4.25 Co-enrollment in other studies is allowed pending approval of the protocol chair.
