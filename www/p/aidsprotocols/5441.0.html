1.0 OBJECTIVES

The primary objective of this study is to evaluate the efficacy and tolerance of two different dosages of fluconazole as prophylaxis for vulvovaginal candidiasis in women with HIV infection at risk of recurrent vaginal infection. This study will also define the epidemiology of recurrent vaginal infection in HIV women and evaluate the mechanism of recurrent infection. The incidences of other fungal infections will also be examined, including oropharyngeal and esophageal candidiasis, dermotophytosis, and deep fungal infections.
