3.1 Inclusion Criteria

3.11 Patients who are seropositive with CD4 cell counts >= 500/mm^3.

3.12 Patients of age >= 13 years.

3.13 Patients must be able and willing to sign an informed consent. Patients who are < 18 years must have written informed consent from a parent or guardian or person with power of attorney.

3.14 HIV infection must be documented by a licensed ELISA.

3.15 Both men and women will be included. Women of childbearing potential should practice adequate birth control to prevent pregnancy while on the study. Women of child bearing potential must have a negative serum or urine Beta HCG within 2 weeks prior to study entry.

3.16 Laboratory parameters as follows:

o Hematology:

o HgB: >= 12 g/dl for males; >= 11 g/dl for females,

o Platelets: >= 100,000/ml,

o Absolute Neutrophil Count (ANC): >= 1000/ml,

o Creatinine: <= 1.5 mg/dl,

o LFT:

o AST/ALT: <= 5 x ULN

o Bilirubin: <= 2.5 x ULN

o Alkaline phosphatase: <= 5 x ULN

3.17 Establishment of EBV transformed B-cell lines: an EBV transformed B-cell line will be established for each study entrant at the time of the initial study list. After successful establishment of each B-cell lines, HIV-1 specific CTL assays will be initiated as described in sections 5.1224 and 5.1225.
