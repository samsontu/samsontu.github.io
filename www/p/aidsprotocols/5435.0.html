5.0 Study Population

HIV positive women presenting with complaints of vaginal discharge, odor, pruritus or dyspareunia will be evaluated for the study. Patients with presumptive diagnosis of vaginal candidiasis will be eligible for the study. A presumptive diagnosis of vulvovaginal candidiasis will be defined as evidence of budding yeasts and pseudohyphae on microscopic examination of a 10% KOH preparation. Final confirmation of the diagnosis is based on positive culture of vaginal swab for Candida spp. Patients without final confirmation of Candida infection will not be evaluable. Patients without final confirmation of Candida infection will not be evaluable. Patients with mixed infections will be treated accordingly (Appendix A).
