6.0 EVALUATION OF EFFECTS

6.1 Primary Evaluation

6.11 Comparison of the slope of CD4 counts over time, pre- and post-emergence of 215 mutation.

6.2 Secondary Evaluation

6.21 A >= 1.0 log change in proviral DNA in CD4 cells or in serum virion RNA rise as measured on two occasions, separated by at least six weeks.

6.22 Change in p24 and beta-2 microglobulin.

6.23 Dose-limiting toxicity.

6.24 Correlation of baseline prognostic markers with development of 215, virologic and immunologic changes.

6.3 Though it is unlikely that statistical significance would be achieved, data will be collected on patients who reach study endpoints (Refer to Section 8.313 and 8.314).
