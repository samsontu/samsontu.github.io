Protocol Synopsis

Objective: To determine the comparative efficacy and tolerance of two different dosages of fluconazole in the treatment of acute vulvovaginal candidiasis in HIV positive women

Design: Randomized, controlled trial

Setting: Multi-center trial involving four university-affiliated medical clinics

Patients: A total of seventy HIV-positive women

Intervention: Patients will receive either 200 mg single dose oral fluconazole or 100 mg fluconazole once daily for seven days.
