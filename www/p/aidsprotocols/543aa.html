Appendix A


Treatment Regimens for Concomitant Infections



1.	Trichomomas vaginitis		Metronidazole 2.0 g PO single dose



2.	Bacterial vaginosis		Metronidazole 500 mg PO BID for seven days



							or



					Clindamycin 300 mg PO BID for seven days



3.	Chlamydia cervicitis		Doxycycline 100 mg PO BID for seven days



							or



					Tetracycline 500 mg QID for seven days



							or



					Erythromycin 500 mg PO QID for seven days



4.	Gonococcal cervicitis		Ceftrioxone 250 mg PO single dose IM



							or



					Ciprofloxacin 500 mg PO single dose
