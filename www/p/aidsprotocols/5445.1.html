5.1 INCLUSION CRITERIA

All of the following must be true:

5.11 HIV positive female.

5.12 Any patient with a history of > or = to 2 episodes of Candida vaginitis (at least one episode documented by a health care specialist) within the past six months before study entry or who have acute vaginal candidiasis within 10 days of study entry.

5.13 Patients must be > or = to 12 years of age. (Parental consent will be obtained from non-emancipated minors less than 18 years of age).

5.14 Women of child-bearing potential should agree to use an effective method of contraception (e.g., condoms and foam) and have a negative pregnancy test at baseline.

5.15 Laboratory parameters:

5.151 Hemoglobin > or = to 8.0 g/dL

5.152 Absolute neutrophil count > or = to 500 cells/mm^3

5.153 Platelets > or = to 50,000/mm^3

5.154 Creatinine < 3X upper limit of normal

5.155 Bilirubin < 3X upper limit of normal

5.156 SGOT (AST), SGPT (ALT) or alkaline phosphatase < 5X upper limit of normal

5.16 Patient is willing to give informed consent to comply with protocol.
