3.2 Exclusion Criteria

3.21 Patients receiving the following medications at time of study entry or within the last 3 months will be excluded from the study:

o Agents with putative immunomodulating activity (i.e.: interferon, steroids, hematopoietins)

o Parenteral therapies (including sc allergy desensitization)

o Other investigational HIV drugs/therapies/vaccines

NOTE: no prior HIV vaccinations are allowed

o Interferon

o Steroids

o Hematopoietins

3.22 Patients suspected of, or diagnosed as having, allergies to any vaccine components.

3.23 Patients who are currently receiving antiretroviral therapy or who have received antiretroviral therapy (ex: ZDV, ddI, or ddC) within the last 6 months.

3.24 Female patients who are pregnant, intend to become pregnant, or who intend to nurse during the study.

3.25 Patients for whom in the opinion of the investigator, there is a medical contraindication or problems in compliance.
