IV. B. EXCLUSIONS

1. Use of experimental anti-retrovirals (other than those available by expanded access protocols) in the past 6 months.

2. Current use of other anti-retrovirals, such as interferon or foscarnet. Acyclovir and gancyclovir [sic] are permitted.

3. Patients receiving chemotherapy for cancer

4. Patients unable to take oral medications

5. Patients hospitalized at the time of enrollment
