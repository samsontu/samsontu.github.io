9.0 DATA COLLECTION AND MONITORING

9.1 Data Collection

Case report forms will be provided for each subject. Patient will be identified by a patient identification number.

9.2 Study Monitoring

Case report forms will be reviewed by the protocol team at UCSD. Data will be entered at UCSD. Patients newly enrolled in the trial should be reported to the monitor at UCSD within seven days: Ben Freeman, phone (619) 543-8080, FAX (619) 298-0177

9.3 Human Subjects

This protocol must receive Institutional Review Board (IRB) approval prior to implementation at each site. Patients should sign an informed consent prior to enrollment.

9.4 Confidentiality

All data related to the patients will be identified by a coded number. Clinical information will be kept confidential to the extent allowed by law.
