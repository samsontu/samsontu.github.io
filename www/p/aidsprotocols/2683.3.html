3.3 Enrollment Procedures

3.31 A total of 370 subjects will be enrolled in this study. Subjects will be stratified by CD4^+ cell counts (greater or less than 100).

3.32 This study will be open to all ACTG sites including pediatric units.

3.33 This protocol is appropriate for prisoner participation.

3.34 There will be no replacement of subjects for premature study discontinuation.

3.35 It is estimated that accrual will be completed within 7-8 months. Enrollment will be assessed after 2 months. If the study is less than 20% accrued, the potential causes for lack of accrual will be assessed and a plan of action formulated and discussed with the Adult Opportunistic Infections Committee.
