IV. A. INCLUSIONS

1. Age >= 18 years

2. Rx with AZT (>= 300 mg/d), ddI (>= 250 mg/d), ddC (>= 1.125 mg/d), or drugs available in expanded access protocols such as d4T, or combinations of AZT/ddC or AZT/ddI continuously for at least 4 of the last 6 months. Subjects must have remained on a single regimen (mono- or combination therapy) for the last 2 months. Thus, for example, a subject who has taken AZT and ddC for 1 year and has switched to d4T 2 months ago is eligible. On the other hand, a subject who has been on AZT and ddC for 1 year and has switched to d4T 1 month ago is not eligible; this patient would become eligible after an additional month of treatment with d4T.

3. Karnofsky's >= 60

4. Able to take oral medications

5. CD4 cell count <= 50 at entry.
