12.0 Sample Size Calculations

The sample size calculations are based on the following estimates of clinical treatment failure: we predict that patients treated with fluconazole 50 mg once daily will have an incidence of clinical treatment failure <5%. Patients who receive weekly fluconazole 200 mg will have an incidence of clinical treatment failure of 40%. At a power of 0.80 and an alpha of 0.05, 64 evaluable patients will be required for study.

Furthermore, the time to clinical relapse or recurrent vaginal infection may be significantly different between the 2 treatment arms.
