5.2 Exclusion criteria (any of the following excludes patient from study)

5.21 Pregnant or nursing women.

5.22 A history of Type I hypersensitivity reaction to fluconazole, ketoconazole, miconazole, clotrimazole or itraconazole (a history of hives, angioedema, wheezing, or anaphylaxis).

5.23 Clinical conditions necessitating oral or intravenous antifungal therapy (with amphotericin, 5-FC, itraconazole or ketoconazole). Topical antifungal therapies, e.g. troches, is discouraged, but allowed.
