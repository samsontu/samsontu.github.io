4.4 ADVERSE EXPERIENCE REPORTING REQUIREMENTS

4.41 Reporting of Adverse Experiences

4.411 Adverse experiences shall be reported to the DAIDS AER Office as described in Appendix III.

4.412 Adverse experiences or deaths requiring immediate reporting shall be made by telephone to 1-800-537-9979 (available 24 hours/day).

4.413 Protocol-Specific Adverse Experience Reporting Requirements

All adverse experiences will be reported to the NIAID Adverse Experience reporting office. Refer to Appendix III. For the Military sites, adverse experiences will be reported to the Military Safety Officer, as well as the NIAID-DAIDS Adverse Experience Office.
