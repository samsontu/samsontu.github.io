On 12 September,1997, I had the chance to stop in London for a day to visit the International Secretariat (IS) of Amnesty International, the headquarter of the world-wide human rights movement. After an exhausing series of meetings with people interested in electronic publishing and the use of the web for human rights activism, we repairedto a nearby pub for some rest and relaxation.
