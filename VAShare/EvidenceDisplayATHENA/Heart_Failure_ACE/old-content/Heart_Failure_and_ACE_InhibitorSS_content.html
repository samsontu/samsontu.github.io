• Patients with left ventricular ejection fraction<36% treated with ACE inhibitors had a substantial reduction in mortality, hospitalization for CHF and reinfarction (table 1) as compared to placebo. (Flather 2000)
  • High dose ACE inhibitor (lisinopril- 30mg) reduced combined outcomes of mortality and hospitalization in 3 years in patients with heart failure as compared to low dose ACE inhibitor (lisinopril 2.5-5mg) (table 2). (Packer 1999)
  • Benefits were observed early after the start of therapy and persisted long term. (Flather 2000)
