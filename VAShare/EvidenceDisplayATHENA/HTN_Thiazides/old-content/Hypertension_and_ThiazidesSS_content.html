• Low dose thiazides ( 25mg/day) reduces risk of death, stroke and coronary heart disease of patients with primary hypertension (Table 1). Wright 1999, Psaty 1997
  • Reduction in systolic blood pressure was significantly greater (by ~2mmHg) with thiazides than with beta adrenergic receptor antagonists or calcium channel blockers. Wright 1999
  • Patients reported a 31% (95% CI 24-37) lower rate of withdrawal due to adverse effects with thiazides as compared with beta adrenergic receptor antagonists. Wright 1999
