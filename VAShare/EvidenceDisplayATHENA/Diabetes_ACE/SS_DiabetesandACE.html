Last Updated: November, 2001

  • Ace inhibitor reduced the risk of stroke, myocardial infarct, or cardiovascular death by 25% in 4.5 years in patients with diabetes and cardiovascular risk factor(s) as compared to placebo. (HOPE 2000)
  • Tight blood pressure control is more important than specific therapy in reducing risk of cardiovascular outcomes in patients with diabetes, and will often require a multidrug regimen.(Sigal 2000; Grossman 2000; UKPDS 38 1998; UKPDS 39 1998; Hansson 1998)
  • The cardiovascular benefit of therapy with ace inhibitor was greater than that attributable to the decrease in blood pressure. (UKPDS 38 1998)
