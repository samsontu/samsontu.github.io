Last Updated: November, 2001

  • Isolated systolic hypertension and wide pulse pressure in elderly persons increase the risk for mortality and morbidity, treatment reduces this risk. (Staessen 2000)
  • Thiazide diuretics (hydrochlorothiazide or chorthalidone) reduced cerebrovascular events by 39%, stroke mortality by 33%, coronary heart disease by 26% and cardiovascular mortality by 25% in 5 years when compared to placebo. (Messerli 1998)
  • Between 46% and 66% of patients will not require additional drug therapy (SHEP 1991; MRC 1992; Messerli 1998) .
  • The higher the patient's baseline risk the greater the benefit of treatment with diuretics.
