Last Updated: November, 2001

  • In a recent meta-analysis to assess the effectiveness of beta blockers in longer term secondary prevention, only metoprolol, propanolol and timolol reduced the odds of death (p<0.05) of patients post MI in long term trials (6-48 months). (Freemantle 1999).
  • Patients receiving current therapeutic options (thrombolytic therapy and aspirin) for MI benefited from beta blocker therapy. (Freemantle 1999)
  • Early initiation of therapy leads to a greater period when benefits may be accrued from therapy. (Freemantle 1999)
